About Us

Welcome to CurbaCraze: Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

CurbaCraze has been a dining go-to place in Buenavista, Marinduque since October 8, 2022. This restaurant is open from 8:00 am to 9:00 pm, with last order at 8:30 pm. From being a simple gotohan good for motorcycle riders, it has grown into this success boasting a multiplicity of bestsellers: Hot and Iced coffees, Milk teas, Calamares, Halo-halo, Samgyup, and Sisig.
